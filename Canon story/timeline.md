## **A.D 2028 | Financial Storm and Corporate Disputes**

In the near future, super corporations have replaced governments as the rulers of the world. Driven by the pursuit of profit, conflicts and wars are incessant.

---

## **A.D 2042 | The Rise of Noah Technocrats**

Amidst this chaotic era, a genius scientist founded Noah Corporation. Leveraging the discovery of a female alien life form "Urd" in outer space, he pioneered the new Dagny technology and rapidly rose to become the world's eleventh super corporation with the formidable power of the single-soldier tactical mecha "Galar."

---

## **A.D 2055 | Astral Dome and the Star Ring War**

With the construction of the strategic-armed, equator-encircling colossal base "Astral Dome," Noah's ambitions posed a significant threat to other factions. Compelled to set aside their previous differences and conflicts, they formed a coalition to launch a comprehensive offensive against Noah Corporation, marking the beginning of the "Star Ring War."

---

## **A.D 2060 | Apocalyptic Upheaval**

However, despite pooling all their strength, the coalition was still no match for the terrifying power of the Astral Dome. In desperation, they ultimately resorted to using super strategic weapons, destroying the Astral Dome at the tragic cost of annihilating most of the Earth's surface ecology.

---

## **A.D 2066 | Human Union and the E.T.E mecha**

On the other hand, through the analysis of "Galar," the coalition finally developed a new type of tactical weapon not constrained by Noah—"E.T.E." They also perfected the pilot transformation surgery known as "Syncer," breaking Noah Corporation's technological advantage. Since then, the top ten forces established the "Human Union," entering a long-term standoff with Noah Corporation.

---

## **A.D 2075 | The Story Begins**

The Human Union's direct subordinate independent military organization — the Ninth Entente — operates from a floating fortress as its base, carrying out global mobile combat missions. One this day, the newly-appointed Nexecutor of the Ninth Entente, escorted by a guard squad, was en route to the base via airship to assume their post. while traversing the shadow zone of the Astral Dome, they were suddenly attacked by an unidentified force. From this moment on, the fate of the world began to change...

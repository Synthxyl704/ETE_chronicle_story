# Izumo Prototype/Nikitama - Stikka
The mecha was custom-tailored by the Nine Entente to the prestigious FFE Akizuki Institute, along with remarkable battlefield survivability, earning it the affectionate nickname Unbreakable Shield among the comrades of Nine Entente.

# Rhine/Covenant - Ruler
The experimental mecha developed by the Nine Entente for Ruler was designed for testing new weaponry. In terms of firepower alone, it stands nearly unrivaled among the Union's E.T.E Gen IV.

# Iwatoshi/Kokuren Type Ⅱ - Yukiwari
Among E.T.Es crafted by the Ninowl for Yukiwari. With this mecha, Yukiwari steps out from being solely a ninja assassin for covert missions, gaining the capability to fight alongside her peers.

# Yu-Heng/Twilight Blaze - Qiqiao
Qiqiao exclusive naval support type E.T.E. Mainly designed to assist team combat. She insists on the pink painting for her mecha, event though teammates has mentioned about bad stealthiness of the color on the battlefields.

# Seablade/Drakeagle - Chisha
Captain Kana generously offered Chisha with the NRA's latest E.T.E, Seablade, enabling her to handle any situation with ease during her journey across the globe in search of clues to the divine water.

# Yao-Guang/Drakonex - Long-ang
The first Gen V mecha in the history of E.T.E. development, custom-crafted for Long-ang by the Ninth Entente. It is also the first to achieve a fully transformable E.T.E. design, making it currently the sole ace mecha capable of handling all types of combat missions.

# Habakkuk/Frost Demon - Crocell
The Abyssal Ship Gear from the old world, which emerged through an unexplained process of autonomous construction. It is theoretically recognized as a special type of E.T.E. This mecha possesses numerous functions governed by enigmatic principles.

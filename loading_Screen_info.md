Frontel City - Has a neutral trading port.

**Astral Dome (AD)** - An orbital structure built by NTC, intended to dominate the world, was ultimately brought to an end by HU in Star Ring War (SRW)

**Commune De Zodia (CDZ)** - Military information and technology development after SRW, almost every HU ETE unit is equipped with information and detection devices developed by Zodia. 

**Golden Lion Republic (GLR)** - The ONLY political entity in the HU, practicing an ancient noble republic system with the most formidable syncer corps in the HU. They also have deep roots in art, literature and history, continues even after SRW.

**Noah Technocrats (NTC)** - Founded by Martin Young in 2042, it quickly became the worlds largest military entity, equipped with cutting edge technology, its automaton legion poses a severe threat to human survival. 

**Far East Energy (FEE)** - Under the territorial waters lie the richest reserves of Dagny particle minerals. The FEE possesses advanced  ETE research capabilities and pioneered the syncer modification surgery. Akizuki Institue is inside FEE.

**MARS Corporation** - Bratz's father and her served there. Strongest  tech and military power in HU, though a part of the HU, MARS is reluctant to be constrained by the Union Council and has been seeking greater influence. 

**Amphis Chamber of Commerce (ACOC)** - A territorial-less faction of 4 giant city ships, engages in trade logistics among major forces due to powerful mobility. The ships are the largest movable hulls in the world, which are now irreproducible due to tech loss from SRW.

**Cona Free State (CFS)** - Faction evolved from religious research groups, integrationg remnants of major sects and adapting plutocratic politics, ultimately forming  a pan-religious group operating as a corporation. 

**Stadling Democratic Union (SDU)** - The HUs major strategic force in the North, ruled by a joint comittee of multiple super-rich conglomerates, SDU possesses the most abundant mineral resources in the remaining land and boasts a highly developed industry.
The Northern part of the SDU faces NTCs base, resulting in prolonged state of partial war.

**Human Union (HU)** - War alliance formed by 10/ten surviving factions after SRW, responsible for defending against NTC, maintaining order and remaining humanity.

**Fortress City** - after SRW, the surviving factions could not maintain border defenses and were forced to relocate the remaining population to fortress cities (plural) with thick walls. 

**Ninth Entente (NE)** - independent military organization nominally part of the HU, operates from large airborne fortresses and is responsible for specialized operations against NTC.

**Nine Regions Alliance (NRA)** - One of core forces of HU, possesses vast land and comprehensive agricultural and industrial capacities, supplying rations and products to nearly half of HUs residents.

**ESW Foundation (ESWF)** - Established by the merger (or "the merging"?) of several financial giants, ESW strives to maintain financial order within the HU amid global financial collapse. 
It sponsors and invests in numerous research institutions, leveraging the technological returns to bolster its military strength.
Based on the ancient royal knights, ESW has formed three knight legions, highly skilled in small-s cale special operations.

**Balde Industrien (BIS)** - A giant industrial group specializing in chemicals, smelting, materials, and machinery manufacturing, with strong capabilities in weapon development, particularly in live ammunition, where it holds a virtual monopoly.
